<h1 align="center">Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard</h1>

<p align="center">
  Interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard for Revenue, Orders, and Operational Performance Analysis
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
This project presents a comprehensive <strong>Sales & Operations Performance Dashboard</strong> built using Amazon marketplace data.
It provides key business insights into revenue trends, order distribution, cancellation rates, shipping performance, and state-level sales analytics.
</p>

<p>
The dashboard enables stakeholders to:
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Monitor total revenue and order volume</li>
  <li>Track cancellation rate and shipping performance</li>
  <li>Analyze revenue contribution by state</li>
  <li>Identify monthly sales trends</li>
  <li>Evaluate operational efficiency</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Dashboard Components</h2>

<h3>1️⃣ Courier Status Distribution</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Visual breakdown of Shipped, Cancelled, and Unshipped orders</li>
  <li>Helps monitor operational performance</li>
  <li>Identifies potential logistics bottlenecks</li>
</ul>

<h3>2️⃣ Top 10 Revenue by State</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Ranks states based on total revenue contribution</li>
  <li>Highlights top-performing regions</li>
  <li>Supports geographic sales strategy decisions</li>
</ul>

<h3>3️⃣ Monthly Revenue & Order Trends</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Tracks revenue growth over time</li>
  <li>Compares order volume against revenue</li>
  <li>Identifies seasonal patterns and demand shifts</li>
</ul>

<h3>4️⃣ Orders by State & Courier Status</h3>
<ul>
  <li>State-level breakdown of shipped, cancelled, and unshipped orders</li>
  <li>Enables operational comparison across regions</li>
  <li>Supports logistics optimization analysis</li>
</ul>

<h2>📌 Business Insights</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Maharashtra and Karnataka are the top revenue-generating states.</li>
  <li>Nearly 10% cancellation rate indicates potential supply chain or payment issues.</li>
  <li>Revenue peaks in April followed by gradual stabilization.</li>
  <li>Shipping performance accounts for ~85%+ successful order fulfillment.</li>
</ul>
